# Task Forge - Design & Branding Document ## 1. Project Overview & North Star **Task Forge** is a high-performance utility ecosystem designed for the modern individual who treats their daily routines like an elite athletic session. It merges the sleek, technical aesthetic of high-end fitness apps with the organized structure of an editorial magazine. **The Mission:** To transform mundane tracking (water, gym, tasks) into a rewarding, visually compelling journey. --- ## 2. Brand Personality - **Technical yet Human:** Precise data visualization balanced with warm, approachable typography. - **Elite but Accessible:** Premium "Pro" features that feel earned, not just bought. - **Kinetic:** Everything feels in motion—progress bars that breathe, cards that shift, and a layout that flows like a workout session. --- ## 3. Visual Language (Kinetic Flow) ### Typography - **Primary:** Lexend (Variable Weight). Chosen for its extreme readability and "modern-technical" feel. Used for headlines and key data points. - **Secondary:** Manrope. Used for body text and navigation labels for a clean, sans-serif balance. ### Color Palette - **Deep Velocity Blue (#0061A4):** Our primary brand color. Used for high-emphasis actions and key branding. - **Forge Orange (#C04000):** Our accent color. Used for "Pro" indicators, active session highlights, and urgent call-to-actions. - **Velocity Slates:** A range of cool grays (from #F8FAFB to #0F172A) for surface depth and separation. ### Shape & Structure - **Roundness:** 8px to 32px (Round Eight to Round Thirty-Two). - **Layout:** Ionic Framework structure (ion-header, ion-content, ion-card). - **Elevation:** Flat architecture with subtle "tonal" shifts and backdrop blurs rather than heavy drop shadows. --- ## 4. Feature Ecosystem ### A. Splash & Onboarding - **The Initializer:** A sleek entrance that sets the tone for an "optimized day." - **Authentication:** Clean, minimalist login with multiple "Collective" entry points (Google, Apple). ### B. Navigation Hub - **The Command Center:** A card-based dashboard that categorizes different "Forges" (Water, Gym, Diet, Sleep). - **Dynamic Headers:** Real-time progress indicators (e.g., "85% Daily Goal Achieved"). ### C. Water Tracker (Hydration Insights) - **Monetary View:** Tracks "Total Expenses" alongside consumption to provide a unique value-based perspective. - **Velocity Bars:** Large, bold indicators for weekly and monthly consumption. ### D. Gym Tracker (Performance Sessions) - **The Forge:** A list-based view of daily routines. - **Detailed Sessions:** A focused view for logging sets, reps, and weights, utilizing Ionic's input components for high-speed entry. ### E. Profile & Stats - **The Athlete Identity:** A centralized view for "Task Forge Elite" members to see their lifetime streaks and manage their account optimization. --- ## 5. Reference Asset Inventory ### IMAGE_4 / IMAGE_5: Workout Whiteboard Schema - **Description:** A handwritten "Level-3" workout plan on a whiteboard. - **Role:** Serves as the source material for the "Gym Tracker" logic (Sets, Reps, and specific exercise lists for Monday-Saturday). - **Key Data Extracted:** - Monday: Chest/Triceps (Flat Press, Inc Press, etc.) - Structure: 3-4 Sets, 8-12 Reps. --- ## 6. Technical Implementation - **Framework:** Ionic Framework. - **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for rapid, consistent utility styling. - **Icons:** Material Symbols / Ionicons.
